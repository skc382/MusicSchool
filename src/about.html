About Us

Our Music School has been established with the purpose of bringing the joy of music to all age groups, young and old alike. Studying and performing music transcends generations and does not have a mandatory retirement age. On the opposite end of the spectrum, some expectant parents participate in prenatal programs that focus on playing music for their unborn child to contribute to the healthy development of their baby. Our point is that music is for everyone! We currently offer classes for preschoolers, and lessons are available for school age children through adults.
Music brings enjoyment to the musicians and to those around them. It is a life-long learning opportunity that is exciting every step of the way. From being able to play your very first song to joining a community ensemble that sparks your interest, each unique experience is yours to treasure. VMS is committed to fostering the musical growth in each student and helping them reach their individual goals, enjoying the journey along the way.
